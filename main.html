Spiti Valley, Himachal Pradesh


One of the best destinations in India for stargazing, Spiti Valley in Himachal Pradesh is just next to heaven for nature lovers and thrill seekers looking for clear starry sky. Rich in natural beauty and cultural heritage, this cold desert mountain valley is among the most immersive places in India. It is often referred to as Little Tibet owing to its strategic location and scenic landscape. This place is indeed where you can have a memorable time interacting with locals and knowing its vibrant culture. Keep it on your Wishlist!
